Studio Protocol

Retainer & Settlement

A non-refundable retainer fee of 70% is strictly required to lock down your specific date and hour during the call week. The remaining 30% balance must be settled completely on the immediate day of your session.

Punctuality & Scheduling

To preserve our seamless back-to-back calendar, sessions are strictly bound to their time limits: 60 minutes for packages 1 & 3, 75 minutes for package 2, and 90 minutes for family tiers. Arrivals extending beyond 20 minutes late automatically attract a fee of ₦15,000.

Selection & Turnaround

Unedited proofs are uploaded directly to Pixieset or selected at the studio post-session. A full download of unwatermarked JPEG proofs is provided for client selection. Final retouched media is delivered within 2 to 4 working days. Unedited RAW files are withheld but can be purchased upon request.

Licensing & Ownership

Clients receive a personal-use license for self-printing and digital publication. Libertypictures retains legal copyright ownership for archive display. Exclusive usage waivers are subject to contract negotiations.
