Notes & Details

Booking & Retainer

To officially reserve your session date and time during call week, a 70% non-refundable retainer is requested upon booking. We kindly ask that the remaining 30% balance is settled completely on the day of your session.

Punctuality & Scheduling

To ensure a seamless experience during our busiest season, sessions are thoughtfully paced: 60 minutes for The Brief & Learned Friends, 75 minutes for The Counsel, and 90 minutes for family tiers. To protect this schedule, arrivals extending beyond 20 minutes late are gently subject to a ₦15,000 adjustment.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

Cancellations do not qualify for refunds. In the case of emergency cancellations or no-show retainer will be forfeited. This ensures fairness to other clients and respects our time and resources. One time rescheduling is allowed free of charge if requested at least 24-48 hours before session. Retainers are valid for 12 months under the right circumstances.

Selection & Turnaround

Proofs are shared via a private Pixieset gallery for your selection. Final retouched media is delivered within 2 to 4 working days via a secure gallery. Unedited RAW files remain safely in our archives, though they can be purchased upon request.

Licensing & Ownership

You receive a personal-use license for self-printing and digital sharing. Libertypictures retains standard copyright ownership for portfolio curation, though privacy requests are always honored.
